About

Lit 4 Linux is a website that intends to educate beginner users on the basics of Linux. Whether you are someone who knows nothing about Linux but wants to learn, or someone who knows some Linux and wants to learn more, our site will both engage and teach you. By using a variety of games surrounding different commands and lessons, Lit 4 Linux hopes to teach people Linux in an entertaining, yet simple way.

Site Goals

LEARN

Lit4Linux covers everything you need to know to get started with Linux. We cover topics from basic to advanced. You will learn to love Linux in no time.

GAMIFICATION

Lit4Linux is built to be interactive with you. Use our fun flashcards when learning new terms.

AUDIENCE

Lit4Linux is for those that need a jump start to their Linux career. Learn Linux with content that is easy to understand and will hold your attention!
